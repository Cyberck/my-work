Hey there! I'm Bakhtiyor Dushayev

My name is Dushayev Bakhtiyor Dekhkanovich. I was born on October 2, 1986, in Gulistan city, Sirdarya region, Uzbekistan. I am married and have three sons. We live happily as a family. We are a big family that includes my mother, my wife, and our three sons.


I graduated with a bachelor's degree and worked in the regional financial department. After many years in business, I bought a house, a car, and a store. However, in 2016, I was hit by a car and sustained 7 fractures. During my recovery, I had time to explore new interests and decided to study programming. In 2017, I started learning to become a programmer on YouTube. I watched various video courses, but there were always some aspects that were unclear. Once I recovered, I began traveling to the capital to study web programming professionally. The course lasted nine months and concluded on December 19, 2021. We studied HTML, CSS, GRID, BOOTSTRAP, GIT, GitHub, JAVASCRIPT, GULP, PUG, SASS, PHP, and VueJS. Currently, I am learning React JS. Additionally, I studied on online platforms like Udemy, Mohirdev, Najot Ta'lim, and Kadirov.dev. Among these, the Kadirovdev course was the best for me, as I learned MySQL, Api-platform, Linux CLI, Design Patterns, PHP, PHP Symfony, phpStorm, Linux Fedora, and Docker. I continue to study and work as a full-stack web programming teacher. I am committed to further development and enjoy writing code all the time. If I don't write code, my hands itch! ðŸ˜…
